AdU Feria Hub is a student-led initiative of the Supreme Student Government (SSG), created to connect skills, ideas, and opportunities. It serves as a platform for AdU students to showcase their talents, explore collaborations, and turn creativity into meaningful work.

Founded by the SSG ABM Representative, and supported by the leadership of the SSG, the project reflects the spirit of collaboration and service for the Adamsonian community.

For inquiries, you may reach us at the Student Affairs Office (SAO), 2nd Floor, Adamson University, San Marcelino St., Manila.

The AdU Feria Hub was initiated by Princess Sarah A. Bernasor, the SSG ABM Strand Representative. Recognizing the need for a structured space where students could promote their products and projects, she spearheaded the creation of this platform. Guided by her leadership and vision, the commission hub was designed to give students the opportunity to showcase their work while fostering community engagement and collaboration.

The website was developed by Nevan Angelo R. Catoy, a dedicated member of the Adamson University Robotics Club. With his skills in website design and programming, he played a vital role in building the platform and ensuring that it could effectively serve the needs of our Klasmeyts. His contribution turned this initiative from an idea into a fully realized digital space for the strand.
